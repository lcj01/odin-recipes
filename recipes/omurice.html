Omurice

Description

Ketchup-flavored chicken fried rice bundled up in a thin layer of tender omelette, Omurice is a classic Japanese home-cooked dish. Every bite is bursting with comforting flavors! No wonder it's so popular among kids and adults alike.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 onion
  • 1 boneless, skinless, chicken thigh
  • extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/2 cup vegetables
  • 1/8 tbsp salt
  • 1/8 tbsp black pepper
  • 2 servings cooked rice
  • 1 tbsp ketchup
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tbsp milk
  • 6 tbsp cheddar cheese

Directions

  1. Ketchup Fried Rice
    1. Mince onion finely into small pieces
    2. Dice chicken thigh into small pieces
    3. Heat pan with extra virgin olive oil
    4. Add and cook the chicken
    5. Add the vegetables, salt, and black pepper
    6. Add the rice
    7. Add ketchup and soy sauce. Stir and combine
  2. Omurice
    1. Whisk 1 egg and milk in a small bowl
    2. Heat pan with extra virgin olive oil
    3. Pour in the egg mixture and coat the pan
    4. Sprinkle cheese in the middle and add fried rice
    5. Fold both sides of the omelette to cover fried rice
    6. Transfer omurice to plate and enjoy
