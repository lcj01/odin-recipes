Dango

Description

Firm, colorful rice balls that are sweet and chewy, this Japanese treat is a must this season. If you like Asian desserts, you should try these yummy mochi donuts and sweet ube ice cream.

Ingredients

  • joshinko glutinous rice flour
  • shiratamako glutinous rice flour
  • powdered sugar
  • hot water
  • matcha powder
  • food coloring

Directions

  1. Soak the skewers in water
  2. Mix the rice flour and the hot water until dough consistency
  3. Divide the dough
  4. Add food coloring to dough
  5. Shape the dough into balls
  6. Bring pot to a boil and cook the balls
  7. Cool the balls and skewer the dango
